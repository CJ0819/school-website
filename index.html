   Welcome

 

   First Galaxy Ridge School is a leading educational institution that provides quality education from preschool to junior high school. The school is dedicated to creating an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. At First Galaxy Ridge School, we believe that every child is unique, and we strive to produce a personalized learning experience that meets the individual needs and interests of each student. Our experienced and dedicated teachers work tirelessly to ensure that our students receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for success in all areas of life.

 

   Our elementary school program builds upon the foundational skills learned in preschool and provides a comprehensive education that prepares students for the challenge of primary and junior high school. Our rigorous academic program is complemented by a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports, music and art, that allows students to explore their intersts and talents outside of the classroom.At First Galaxy Ridge School, our students are challenged to reach their full potential academically while also developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

 

   At First Galaxy Ridge School, we are committed to providing a safe and nurturing environment where students can learn and grow their full potential. We believe that education is key to unlocking a bright and successful future, and we are proud to be a part of the educational journey of our students.
