   About us



  First Galaxy Ridge School is a leading educational institution that provides quality education from preschool to junior high school. The school is dedicated to creating an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. At First Galaxy Ridge School, we believe that every child is unique, and we strive to produce a personalized learning experience that meets the individual needs and interests of each student. Our experienced and dedicated teachers work tirelessly to ensure that our students receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for success in all areas of life.


  Our elementary school program builds upon the foundational skills learned in preschool and provides a comprehensive education that prepares students for the challenge of primary and junior high school. Our rigorous academic program is complemented by a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports, music and art, that allows students to explore their intersts and talents outside of the classroom.At First Galaxy Ridge School, our students are challenged to reach their full potential academically while also developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills. The facilities at First Galaxy Ridge School are second to none, with well-equipped classrooms, a state-of-the-art library, a fully equipped science laboratory and a computer laboratory.


  At First Galaxy Ridge School,we are committed to providing a safeand nurturing environment where students can learn and grow their full potential. We believe that education is a partnership between the school, students and parents. We encourage parents to be actively involved in their childs education and work with them to ensure that their child receives the best possible education. "Education is key to unlocking a bright and successful future."


  Admissions

Thank you for considering our school for your child's education. We would be delighted to have you and your child join our community of learners. In order to apply for admission to our school, you will need to contact our administration office for more information. Our administrative team will be happy to provide you with all the necessary details regarding the admission process, including the required documents, fees, and deadlines. We take pride in offering a diverse and inclusive learning environment that caters to the unique needs of each child. Our curriculum is designed to foster creativity, critical thinking, and social-emotional development, enabling children to reach their full potential. Please note that we start the admission process from as young as 3 months old, and we accept applications throughout the year. We recommend that you apply as early as possible to ensure that your child has the best chance of securing a spot in our program. Once again, thank you for considering our school. We look forward to hearing from you and welcoming your child to our community of learners.
