   Departments


  Preschool


We believe that each child is unique and has their own set of talents and abilities. Our program is designed to foster a love of learning by providing a safe, fun, and nurturing environment where people can explore, discover, and create.

We use play-based activities to promote social, emotional, physical, and cognitive development and encourage children to become active participants in their own learning.

Our dedicated and experienced teachers are committed to providing a quality education that sets the foundation for future success. We look forward to partnering with you and your child on this exciting educational journey.








  Primary


We strive to provide well-rounded education that prepares our students for success in all aspects of life. Our curriculum is designed to engage students in hands-on experiential learning that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration.

We believe in promoting a growth mindset, where students are encouraged to take risks, learn from mistakes, and persist in the face of challenges.

Our dedicated teachers are committed to providing a supportive and inclusive learning environment that nurtures each child's unique strengths and talents. We look forward to partnering with you and your child to create a meaningful and fulfilling educational experience.







  JHS


We aim to provide a challenging and comprehensive education that prepares our students for success in high school and beyond. Our curriculum is designed to provide a strong foundation in core academic subjects while also fostering critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration skills.

We believe in promoting a growth minset, where students are encouraged to take ownership of their learning and set high expectations for themselves. Our experienced teachers are dedicated to providing a rigorous and supportive learning environment that challenges and inspires each student to reach their full potential.
